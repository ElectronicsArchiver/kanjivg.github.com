Here is a list of projects using KanjiVG. If you want your project to appear here, please send a mail to our mailing list! Projects are listed by chronological order of use.

  • Tagaini Jisho Japanese dictionary and study assistant.
  • sci.lang.japan online handwritten kanji recognition
  • Tegaki, Open Source Chinese and Japanese handwriting recognition.
  • HTML5 Kanji stroke animations by Julien Quint (uses older version of the data).
  • HTML5 Stroke Animation, cool animated writing of any Japanese text.
  • Jisho.org dictionary, a great online Japanese/kanji dictionary.
  • OBenkyo, an android application to study hiragana, katakana and kanji.
  • http://kanji.prasatko.jp/, a Czech Japanese and Kanji dictionary.
  • IDSgrep, a kanji search program in the spirit of grep.
